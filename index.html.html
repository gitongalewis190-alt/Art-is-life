The Artist's Journey

The Untold Canvas — My Origin

From a young age, I was drawn to creativity in a way I could not fully explain. I didn't just want to draw — I wanted to express feeling, emotion, and meaning through every line.

Everything changed when I attended the Africa Kidpreneur Summit. I met young creatives turning their ideas into real opportunities. That experience opened my mind: art is not just passion — it can become purpose, identity, and livelihood.

"Art for me is not just life — it is evidence of life itself."

How It All Started — My Untold Story

My Identity — CEO of Art Is Life

This is who I am. Art Is Life Foundation is not just a brand �� it is my identity, my voice, and my purpose. As its CEO and founder, every decision I make, every stroke I paint, carries the weight of who I am and who I am becoming.

The Beginning — Early Artworks & Foundation

From a young age, I was drawn to creativity in a way I could not fully explain. I didn't just want to draw; I wanted to express feeling, emotion, and meaning through every line.

The Turning Point — Africa Kidpreneur Summit & Elvis

A defining moment came at the Africa Kidpreneur Summit. Elvis introduced us to the foundations of painting — how to blend colors, control the brush, and most importantly, how to express emotion through art. That moment changed everything.

"That moment changed how I saw creativity — it was no longer just a skill, but a language."

The Vision — What I Aim to Build & Become

I see a future where my art reaches the world — galleries, collections, conversations across continents. Art Is Life Foundation is my dream institution: one that uplifts artists across Kenya.

Current Work — Present Skill, Progress & Maturity

This is where I stand today. The progress is real. The skill is sharper. Each piece now carries not just emotion, but mastery. Today, my work tells human stories that words cannot fully capture.
