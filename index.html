How It All Started — My Story

My Identity

Art Is Life Foundation is my identity, my voice, and my purpose. As its CEO and founder, every decision I make carries the weight of who I am.

The Beginning

From a young age, I was drawn to creativity in a way I could not fully explain. I didn't just want to draw; I wanted to express feeling through every line.

The Turning Point

The Africa Kidpreneur Summit changed everything. I learned that art is not just passion — it can become purpose, identity, and livelihood.

"Art for me is not just life — it is evidence of life itself."
