The Artist's Journey

The Untold Canvas — My Origin

From a young age, I was drawn to creativity in a way I could not fully explain. I didn't just want to draw — I wanted to express feeling, emotion, and meaning through every line.

Everything changed when I attended the African Kidpreneur Summit. I met young creatives turning their ideas into real opportunities. That experience opened my mind: art is not just passion — it can become purpose, identity, and livelihood.

"Art for me is not just life — it is evidence of life itself."
Lewis Gitonga · CEO & Founder, Art Is Life Foundation

How It All Started — My Untold Story

My Identity — CEO of Art Is Life

Art Is Life Foundation is not just a brand — it is my identity, my voice, and my purpose. As its CEO and founder, every decision I make, every stroke I paint, carries the weight of who I am and who I am becoming.

The Beginning — Early Artworks & Foundation

From a young age, I wanted something deeper — to express feeling, emotion, and meaning through every line. These early works represent the raw, honest foundation of my creative journey.

The Turning Point — Kitmanua Summit & Elvis

At the Kitmanua Summit, Elvis influenced and guided my artistic growth. He introduced the foundations of painting — how to blend colors, control the brush, and express emotion. A mentor who believed in the art before I fully did.

"That moment changed how I saw creativity — it was no longer just a skill, but a language."

The Vision — What I Aim to Build

I see a future where my art reaches the world — galleries, collections, conversations across continents. Art Is Life Foundation is my dream institution: one that uplifts artists across Kenya and proves African art belongs everywhere.

Current Work — Present Skill & Maturity

This is where I stand today. The progress is real. The artistry more mature. Each piece carries not just emotion, but mastery — telling human stories that words cannot fully capture.

"Art for me is not just life — it is evidence of life itself."
