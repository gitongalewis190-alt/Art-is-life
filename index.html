HOW IT ALL STARTED – BY LEWIS THE CEO

From a young age, I found myself deeply drawn to creativity and crafting. In school, while many of my peers focused on competitive drawing—especially cars—I developed a strong desire not only to participate, but to improve, excel, and master skill through dedication.

My turning point came when I met Elvis, who introduced painting as expression. That moment changed everything.

I began self-teaching beyond structure, focusing on emotion, not just technique.

A breakthrough came at the African Kidpreneur Summit where I saw art become opportunity.

That moment marked the birth of Art Is Life Foundation.

Today, I create not just to paint—but to build a future where art becomes identity and life itself.
