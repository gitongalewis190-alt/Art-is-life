About the Artist

I am a Kenyan contemporary artist working under the initiative Art Is Life Foundation. My work explores emotional depth, human psychology, identity, and lived experience through painting. Each piece is created to be felt as much as it is seen, capturing silent emotions, cultural memory, and personal reflection.

Beyond personal creation, I envision building a foundation that provides space for upcoming artists to grow, access visibility, and develop work that is both affordable and communicative.

Through art, I have learned that everything meaningful is built slowly—stroke by stroke. The end of something often reveals what the beginning could never show, yet every stage carries value.

I am continuously growing, refining my process, and optimizing my creative potential with each piece.
