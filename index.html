About the Artist

My Story

I am Lewis Gitonga, a painter based in Kenya. I believe that art is not decoration — it is a language that speaks when words fail.

Each canvas I create carries emotion, memory, and intention. The Art Is Life Foundation exists to share these works with the world, and to prove that African creativity deserves its place on the global stage.

I work primarily in oils and acrylics, drawing inspiration from the landscapes, people, and stories of East Africa — filtered through an abstract lens that invites each viewer to find their own meaning.

Every piece in this collection is original, hand-signed, and comes with a certificate of authenticity.
